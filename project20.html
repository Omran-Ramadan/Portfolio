Al Tarfa Art Center

Sharjah

The Art Center is designed as an open, community-oriented space that blends art creation and exhibition.
Its concept focuses on transparency, using light-filled studios, flexible galleries, and a central atrium to connect visitors with the creative process.
A simple material palette and passive lighting strategies enhance sustainability.
The result is a welcoming cultural hub that supports creativity, learning, and public engagement.
